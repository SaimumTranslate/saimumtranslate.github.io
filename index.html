Welcome to Saimum Translate!

Saimum Translate is a non-profit organization. The purpose of the organisation is to translate Saimum Series into English language and spread the series all over the world.

How it works

Saimum Translate is a volunteer based organization. Any person who want to support us in order to continue to translate Saimum Series can be a member of the organization. The members are, thus, divided into two main part: a) Supporter and b) Contributor. Then, contributors are also divided into another two halves: a) Translator and b) Proof-Reader. A translator translates Saimum Series while a proof-reader just proof-reads the work. A translator can either translate or proof-read while a proof-reader can only proof-read the work and can suggest a improved translation or advice. When translation is done for a chapter, the chapter is added to the repository immediately.

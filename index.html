Translating Saimum Series...

Welcome to Saimum Translate!

Saimum Translate is a non-profit organization. The purpose of the organisation is to translate Saimum Series into English language and spread the series all over the world.

How it works

Saimum Translate is a volunteer based organization. Any person who want to support us in order to continue to translate Saimum Series can be a member of the organization. The members are, thus, divided into two main part: a) Supporter and b) Contributor. Then, contributors are also divided into another two halves: a) Translator and b) Proofreader. A translator translates Saimum Series while a proofreader just proofreads the work. A translator can either translate or proofread while a proofreader can only proofread the work and can suggest a improved translation or advice. When translation is done for a chapter, the chapter is added to the repository immediately.

Want to contribute

If you want to contribute, all you need is a facebook account. If you have one, just join our facebook group. If you do not have one, just use our repository to create your own version and send the repository link via email. You may be also added to the organization based on your performance.
